April 26, 2021

I was in a room with two friends that I have not talked to for a long time. The room was painted in white, with black stairs leading to a red door. I went up the stairs and rotated the handle, but it was stuck.

This is a locked room.

I looked around the room and there was nothing else. This is a completely white, empty room. At this point I heard some buzzing sounds behind me. I turned around and saw my two friends talking to each other. Their voices were so blurred that I could not catch a word, and they seemed to be indifferent to the fact that we were locked. Then I stared up at the ceiling. How strange it was that the room was bright without any light in it.


April 30, 2021

I was standing in a train station. The station was in a simple modern style with rectilinear structure. I heard someone keeping repeating the word “station” in English.

Station, station, station, station, station...

I felt like I just ended something big, and I met some other people in this dream. But the memory of their identites and our interactions was lost.

All I remembered was the sound of “station” all around me.


May 3, 2021

I saw a cabinet, and some clothes hangers next to it. My black mug was on the cabinet, which reminded me that this place was my bedroom in my apartment.

Everything was in their usual place. Everything except the strange mirrors around the cabinet, which were not suppposed to be in my room. A large rectangular mirror was hung above the cabinet, a smaller one was placed on it, and a round dressing mirror by the side of it. So many mirrors that I began to have a feeling of insecurity from exposing myself to them.

Did I look into the mirrors? I did not remember.

Then I noticed that above the largest mirror there were two switches that were too high for me to reach.

What were they for? I kept having this question in my mind as I wandered around in this room that I was both familiar and unfamiliar with.


May 5, 2021

I was walking in a park that looked very similar to Central Park. The trees and roads took me back to New York City.

I walked down a road to a small lake, and found a bench nearby to sit on. I looked into the lake and saw countless thick black “ribbons” moving rapidly. They were twisted together like snakes, but I could not tell whether they were living creatures or not.


May 8, 2021

Trees.

I was walking in trees in beautiful golden sunset.

This drean was very peaceful, like a meditation, although the part that I still remembered when I woke up was very short.


May 11, 2021

I dreamt of the house that I lived when I was a kid. I dreamt of the table where my dad put his orchid on.

I dreamt of a plastic bottle of my favorite drink.

I dreamt of an apple. My mom always prepared an apple before I went to school when I was a kid.

And I dreamt of the bamboos in the house.
